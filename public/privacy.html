Sheet Cards Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Data We Access

  • Basic Google account authorization for sign-in.
  • Spreadsheet files you create with this app or explicitly select in this app.
  • Card data and learning statistics stored in your selected spreadsheet.

How We Use Data

  • Load your flashcards for study sessions.
  • Write back study progress and mastery statistics.
  • Store recent sheet references only in your local browser storage for convenience.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your sheet content with third parties for advertising.
  • We do not run a backend that stores your sheet content in this app version.

Your Controls

  • Disconnect Google access in the app at any time.
  • Clear local app data (recent sheets and local settings) from the app.
  • Remove app access from your Google account security settings.
